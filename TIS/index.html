Activities at the Temple

You can enjoy activities at the temple with your family and loved ones. The purpose for the temple is to serve those that cannot serve themselves and why not enjoy the natural blessings that being in the presence of the temple can bring to your life? Enjoy walking, sharing time with colleagues, having a family picnic, enjoying the beautiful landscape, and more. Once you are inside, experience the beauty throughout the temple from the baptistry to the celestial rooms.
