Scenarios

  1. Persona 1
    1. What is the weather like today? Can I sneak away and drop my line?
    2. What is the 3 / 5 / 7-day forecast? My wife is out with the kids at school, can I get away in the next 3 days?
    3. What are the local new stories? My wife told me that someone was found floating in the river! Did they find my hole?
  2. Persona 2
    1. When we go through the area, will it be snowing again! I dislike snow.
    2. The kids want to have lunch at a park, will we need coats, rain gear, or maybe some sunscreen?
